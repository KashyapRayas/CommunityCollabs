1. What is the Web Project?

The Siggraph BNMIT Web Project is a club website revamp currently being undertaken
by a team of Web designers of Siggraph BNMIT.

We plan to create a unique, creative and visually stunning experience for
students, club members & visitors.

1. What is Community Collab?

Community Collab is a part of the Web Project, where the community of Siggraph BNMIT helps decide
on various aspects of the website such as music, theme, art style and web features.

Collabs help us design a better experience for students, club members & visitors by
by driving community input & feedback into/while simultaneously designing the website.

New Community Collabs will be launched frequently (every 1-2 weeks) on our social media pages
and here in this website.
